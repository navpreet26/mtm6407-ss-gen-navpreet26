The Vibrant Culture of Punjab

November 26, 2024

Punjab is known for its vibrant culture, with lively folk dances like Bhangra and Gidha, and festivals such as Vaisakhi, which celebrate the region's agricultural heritage and strong community spirit.

The celebrations reflect a deep connection to the land and the importance of family and tradition in Punjabi life.
